Indian Consortium for Interactive Digital Narratives

The Indian Consortium for Interactive Digital Narratives (ICIDN) envisions India as a dynamic hub for the creation, study, and critical engagement of interactive digital narratives across diverse media forms. By fostering collaboration among artists, scholars, designers, and technologists, the consortium seeks to advance both creative practice and academic inquiry in domains such as electronic literature, video games, digital storytelling, visual arts, and digital theatre. ICIDN aspires to cultivate an inclusive and innovative ecosystem that not only reflects India’s rich cultural and linguistic plurality but also contributes meaningfully to global dialogues on digital creativity, narrative design, and the evolving relationship between technology and human expression.
